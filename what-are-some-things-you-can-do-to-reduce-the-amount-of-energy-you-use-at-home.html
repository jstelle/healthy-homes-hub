What Are Some Things You Can Do to Reduce the Amount of Energy You Use at Home?

At home, you can reduce your energy usage by making small changes in your lifestyle. Reducing your energy usage will also help you save money on your energy bills. There are many things you can do at home to reduce your energy consumption. Here are some ideas to help you reduce your energy usage at home: Turn off lights when they aren’t being used. This can help you reduce the amount of energy used by lighting in your home. Lights use a lot of energy, even when they’re not on. It’s estimated that lighting accounts for about 10 percent of the average household’s energy costs. By turning off lights when they aren’t being used, you can reduce your energy costs by about 10 percent. Use natural light whenever possible. Install a programmable thermostat

A programmable thermostat is one of the easiest and most effective ways to reduce your home’s energy usage. By running the HVAC system less frequently, you can reduce your energy usage.
